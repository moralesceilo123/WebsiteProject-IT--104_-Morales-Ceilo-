HTML/BOOTSTRAP

In my HTML/Bootstrap project, I use a combination of grid and flexbox systems to organize and layout content on web pages. It's like arranging items on a grid or moving them around flexibly to create a visually appealing design. With Bootstrap's grid system, I can divide the page into rows and columns, making it easy to place content exactly where I want it. Flexbox, on the other hand, gives me more control over how elements behave and align within those grid sections, allowing for dynamic and responsive layouts. Together, they make building websites efficient and enjoyable, like solving a puzzle where every piece fits perfectly into place.
